Our project

Looking for a place to stay as a student is no easy task. On Facebook alone there are already dozens of different groups that offer rooms for students. That is why we decided to combine all of those rooms in one central place: Trivakamer.

Trivakamerâ€™s main goal is to make it easier and more efficient for students to find student housing by allowing them to filter and sort by certain criteria (surface of room, price, etc...). Therefore, our website has filter mechanisms to allow for an easy and pleasant searching experience. To get the best experience for our students, Trivakamer will be free to use, and even creating a free(!) account on the website will be optional, since almost all of the features will be available for users that do not have an account as well.

By making it easier for students to find rooms we hope to reduce the student housing shortage in the Netherlands, which will benefit both the students and the landlords.
